Early Life

Albert Einstein was born in Ulm, Germany, in 1879. He was a curious child and showed early signs of interest in mathematics and science.

Education

Einstein studied at the Polytechnic Institute in Zurich, Switzerland, where he developed his interest in theoretical physics.
